IEEE MSIT Student Branch and IEEE EDS MSIT Student Branch Chapter began its journey in July 2015. It was established by two faculty members of ECE Department, with assistance from 12 students of the Department. ECE Department currently boasts of 2 Student Branch Chapters, IEEE EDS MSIT SBC and IEEE COMSOC MSIT SBC. The Branch has gone a long way since then and currently boasts of 4 Student Branch Chapters.IEEE MSIT Student Branch and ECE Department have organized IEEE International Conference, EDS Minicolloquium, EDS DL Talks, Seminars, workshops, Technical Talks, Industry Academia meets for the benefit of students.
